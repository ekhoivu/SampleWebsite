My Story So Far

"Fate cannot be sidestepped or outrun" - Dean Koontz. I have been into computers for as long as I could remember. At a very young age, I was taught to build computers by my father. By middle school, I had learned Pascal and was part of the school's programming team. Technology was our family DNA, with my father being the IT manager for Ericsson Vietnam and my mother, the Deputy Director General of VietNam Telecommunications Authority. Yet, as I entered highschool, I opted to explore the business world instead, choosing Finance as my future.
At the age of 17, I left home to embark on a decade-long journey that would take me through seven cities across four countries and three continents. Funnily enough, 15 years after that decision, technology is again all I can think about. As we settled in Canada, I am back picking up where I left off, pursuing my childhood dream and becoming the person I have always been destined to be.
